Peak Shaving

Reduce your electricity bills by cutting down power usage during peak demand hours. Solar panels, combined with battery storage, allow you to use stored solar energy when grid electricity is most expensive ‚Äî saving costs and reducing grid stress.

Demand Response

Participate in demand response programs by using your solar and battery system to adjust energy usage during high grid demand. This not only helps stabilize the grid but also gives you potential incentives and energy credits.

Load Shifting

Shift your power usage from high-tariff times to solar-powered or off-peak periods. With intelligent solar systems and storage, you can manage loads efficiently ‚Äî running appliances when solar generation is high or grid rates are low.
